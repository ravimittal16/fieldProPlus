Details

This tutorial will cover the 3 main topics in the time card module.

1. ‘Clock In’ and ‘Clock Out’.

    This is the mechanism used to indicate the time you start your work day to the time you finish your work day.

    At the beginning of each day select today’s date and click on the ‘Clock In’ button

    At the end of each day click on the ‘Clock Out’ button

    Note: If you forgot to ‘Clock Out’ for the previous day, notify your administrator so the time may be manually corrected. Clicking on the 'Clock Out' button after the actual 'Clock Out' time, would result in the incorrect time being reported.

2. ‘Check In’ and ‘Check Out’.

    This is the mechanism used to record the details of the work that was performed during the work day.

    Each job or tasks that is performed is indicated by ‘Check In’ and ‘Check Out’.

    ‘Check In’ and ‘Check Out’ is placed within the ‘Clock In’ and ‘Clock Out’.

    From a work order, there are 2 places from where time can be automatically added to the timecard

    By clicking on the ‘In Route’ button in a work order ... a ‘Check In’ and ‘Check Out’ is automatically added to the Time card. (view image below)

    Time may also be automatically added to the time card by clicking on the ‘Check In’ and ‘Check Out’ button in a work order


    Adding time manually to a time card: This is an important feature that needs to be followed in order to maintain time card integrity. Often you are asked to do work that is not in your work orders. This work maybe verbally assigned to you from your office. The best way to capture this work in the time card is to use the 'Add Time' button as shown below.

    Note: It is important that all fields need to be filled in the 'Add Time' window. You may need to ask your office administrator what job code and Work Order number needs to be selected. Additionally 'Add Time' should also be used to record any non work related task that you performed... like taking lunch, or going to a doctor's visit etc.

3. Approving time card.

    Each day’s time card needs to be submitted for Approval.

    The administrator may Approve or Reject a time card. If the time card is rejected by the administrator, the time card will be given a status of 'Rejected' with a note for why it was rejected. You can then update the time card note and submit again for Approval.
